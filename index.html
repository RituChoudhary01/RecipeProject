Gulab Jamun

Ingredients

  • Milk Powder
  • Flour
  • Sugar
  • Ghee
  • Cardamom

Recipe

  1. Make a dough with milk powder, flour, and ghee.
  2. Shape into small balls.
  3. Deep fry until golden brown.
  4. Soak in sugar syrup.

Rasgulla

Ingredients

  • Milk
  • Lemon Juice
  • Water
  • Sugar

Recipe

  1. Boil milk and add lemon juice to curdle.
  2. Strain and knead the chhena.
  3. Shape into balls and cook in sugar syrup.

Barfi

Ingredients

  • Milk Powder
  • Condensed Milk
  • Ghee
  • Cardamom Powder
  • Nuts

Recipe

  1. Mix milk powder, condensed milk, and ghee.
  2. Cook until it thickens.
  3. Spread on a plate and let it set.
  4. Cut into squares and garnish with nuts.

Jalebi

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Yogurt
  • Sugar
  • Saffron
  • Ghee

Recipe

  1. Make a thick batter with flour and yogurt.
  2. Ferment for several hours.
  3. Pipe into hot oil in spiral shapes.
  4. Dip in saffron-flavored sugar syrup.

Laddu

Ingredients

  • Besan (Gram Flour)
  • Ghee
  • Sugar
  • Cardamom Powder
  • Nuts

Recipe

  1. Roast besan in ghee until golden brown.
  2. Add sugar and cardamom powder.
  3. Mix well and let it cool slightly.
  4. Shape into small round laddus and garnish with nuts.
